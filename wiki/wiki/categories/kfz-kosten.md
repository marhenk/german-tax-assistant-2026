# KFZ-Kosten (Vehicle Expenses)

*Updated: 2025-05-27 | Tax Year: 2023-2026 | Reliability: ⭐⭐⭐⭐*

## Summary
Business vehicle expenses: fuel, insurance, repairs, leasing. Complex category due to private-use rules.

## Legal Basis
- § 4 Abs. 5 Nr. 6 EStG (mixed-use vehicles)
- § 6 Abs. 1 Nr. 4 EStG (1% rule for company cars)
- Effective: Ongoing
- Applies to: Businesses with vehicles (owned or leased)

## Rules
### Owned Vehicle (100% Business Use)
- **Deductible**: Fuel, insurance, repairs, depreciation
- **Fahrtenbuch required**: To prove 100% business use

### Owned Vehicle (Mixed Use)
- **Two methods**:
  1. **Fahrtenbuch**: Deduct actual business %
  2. **1% rule**: Deduct all costs, add 1% of list price as private income monthly

### Leased Vehicle
- **Leasing payments**: Deductible (business % only if mixed use)
- **Other costs**: Fuel, insurance (same rules as owned)

## Edge Cases
- **Electric vehicles**: 0.25% rule (reduced from 1% for BEV <€60k list price, 2020-2030)
- **Hybrid (plug-in)**: 0.5% rule (if battery >40km range or CO₂ <50g/km)
- **Company car charging at home**: Flat €30/month (BEV) or €70/month (hybrid) added income if no Fahrtenbuch
- **Bicycle/E-Bike**: 100% deductible if business use proven, no private use taxation

## Counter-Arguments
- **Fahrtenbuch vs. 1% rule**: Tax advisors often recommend Fahrtenbuch for low private use (<10%), 1% rule for high private use
- **Depreciation**: Some argue for full depreciation in year 1 (§7g EStG investment deduction) — consult tax advisor

## Examples
```
Fuel (100% business): €100 + €19 USt = €119
→ Category: KFZ-Kosten
→ Amount: €100 (net)
→ Vorsteuer: -€19
```

```
Leasing (50% business use, Fahrtenbuch): €500 + €95 USt = €595
→ Category: KFZ-Kosten
→ Amount: €250 (50% of net)
→ Vorsteuer: -€47.50 (50% of USt)
```

```
1% rule (company car, list price €40,000):
→ Monthly private use income: €400 (1% of €40,000)
→ Annual: €4,800 added to Betriebseinnahmen
→ All KFZ costs 100% deductible
```
