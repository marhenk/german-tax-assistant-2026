# Raumkosten (Office/Rent Costs)

*Updated: 2025-05-27 | Tax Year: 2023-2026 | Reliability: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐*

## Summary
Office rent, utilities, property tax. Includes home office (Arbeitszimmer) with strict rules.

## Legal Basis
- § 4 Abs. 5 Nr. 6b EStG (home office deduction limits)
- § 9 Abs. 5 EStG (employee home office — not applicable for EÜR)
- Effective: Ongoing
- Applies to: All businesses with dedicated office space

## Rules
### External Office (Rented/Owned)
- **Fully deductible**: Rent, utilities, property tax, insurance
- **Net amounts**: Record net cost (exclude Vorsteuer if deductible)

### Home Office (Arbeitszimmer)
- **Strict conditions**:
  1. **Dedicated room**: Exclusively business use (no bed, TV, personal items)
  2. **Center of business**: If home is primary work location → 100% deductible (max €1,250/year before 2023, no limit from 2023)
  3. **No other office available**: If no external office → limited deduction

- **2023+ rules** (Corona-driven changes):
  - **Home office days**: €6/day (max €1,260/year) — no dedicated room required
  - **Or** traditional Arbeitszimmer rules (dedicated room, no limit if center of business)

## Edge Cases
- **Partial room use**: Not deductible (must be separate room)
- **Mixed residential/business property**: Deduct business % (by square meters)
- **Coworking space**: Fully deductible (same as external office)
- **Storage/archive room**: Deductible if exclusively business (no living use)

## Counter-Arguments
- **BMF vs. courts**: Some courts allow partial deductions for larger rooms used primarily for business — BMF rejects this, consult tax advisor
- **Home office days vs. Arbeitszimmer**: Choose method annually (can't mix in same year)

## Examples
```
External office rent: €1,000 + €190 USt = €1,190
→ Category: Raumkosten
→ Amount: €1,000 (net)
→ Vorsteuer: -€190
```

```
Home office (Arbeitszimmer, 20m² of 100m² apartment):
→ Rent: €1,500/month → Business portion: €300 (20%)
→ Utilities: €200/month → Business portion: €40 (20%)
→ Category: Raumkosten
→ Monthly total: €340
→ Annual: €4,080 (fully deductible if center of business)
```

```
Home office days (no dedicated room): 220 workdays
→ €6/day × 220 = €1,320
→ Max deduction: €1,260 (capped)
→ Category: Raumkosten
→ No Vorsteuer (lump sum)
```
